## Locale
The CCX SDK lets you customize the locale during initialization. The default value is 'en_US'. 
| Locale | Value (string)
| :-- | :--
| English | en_US
| French  | fr_FR
| German  | de_DE
| Japanese | ja_JP
| Italian | it_IT
| Spanish | es_ES
| Portuguese | pt_BR
| Korean  | ko_JR
| Danish  | da_DK
| Dutch   | nl_NL
| Norwegian | nb_NO
| Swedish | sv_SE
| Finnish | fi_FI
| Chinese (simplified) | zh_Hans_CN
| Chinese (traditional)| zh_Hant_TW
